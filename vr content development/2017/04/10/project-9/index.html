Google Expeditions

Working at Vida Systems, Mike had the amazing opportunity to work with Google on their Expeditions project. Google Expeditions is a VR learning experience designed for classrooms. With just a smartphone and a Cardboard viewer, students can journey to far-away places and feel completely immersed in the environment. Mike is the art director at Vida Systems for this project.
